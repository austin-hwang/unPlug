How To

Create your first alarm! To do so, you must set the following:

  1. Set a time (HH:MM) and time period (AM or PM)
  2. Select a charity to donate to when snooze is pressed (from the dropdown menu of 4 choices)
  3. Select an amount to donate each time you press snooze (in USD)
  4. Upload an image of an object far away from your bed that will need to be compared to when turning off the alarm.
  5. Wait for the alarm to ring â€¦ Maybe take a nap during this time.
  6. When the alarm rings, choose whether to turn it off by taking a photo of the object you initially uploaded (far away from your bed, requiring you to get up) OR snooze.

Beware: Snoozing sends money to the charity you chose!
